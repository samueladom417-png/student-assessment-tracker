About TeachTrack Ghana

Empowering Ghanaian Teachers with Digital Assessment Tools

Our Mission

To provide Ghanaian teachers with a simple, efficient, and accessible digital tool for tracking student assessments, reducing paperwork, and improving educational outcomes.

Key Features

  • Easy Assessment Management - Add, edit, and delete student assessments
  • Automatic Grading - Instant percentage and grade calculation
  • Visual Progress Tracking - Color-coded grades and performance bars
  • Offline Capability - Works without internet connection
  • Data Export - Download assessments as CSV for reports
  • Mobile-Friendly - Optimized for smartphones and tablets

Designed for Ghana

This app is specifically created with Ghanaian teachers in mind:

Based on Ghana's grading system
Includes Ghanaian curriculum subjects
Optimized for mobile data usage
Works in areas with poor internet

About the Developer

Teach Track Ghana was created by a 16yrs old Student Of Prempeh College who is passionate about improving the quality of Education through Technology

Note: This app is completely free to use. No ads, no subscriptions, no data collection. Your assessment data stays securely on your device.
